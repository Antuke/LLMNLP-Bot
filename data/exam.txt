Information about exams:
The LLM and NLP exam consists of a project work and an oral test, assessing both practical application and theoretical understanding.
The project work requires students to apply the methodologies learned in the course to a real-world case, demonstrating critical thinking and implementation skills.
The oral test evaluates theoretical knowledge, the ability to justify design choices made in the project, and responses to questions on course topics.
The final grade is the average of the marks obtained in both assessments.
The date of for the deliver of the project is the 18th of February, while the oral examination will be held the 21th of February.
